Seattle Logistic Services

Ocean Freight

We work with all the major ports in US and Canada and all the Steamship Lines offering service by ocean for most type of cargo. LCL, FCL, Ro-Ro, Breakbulk, Vehicles, Boats, Construction Equipment, Mining, Oil/Gas, Forestry etc

Export

Specializing in Export Shipments we will deliver your freight from the suppliers door to any destination worldwide. Export Declarations, Vehicle Export Title Clearance and all documentation handling.

Trucking

From small shipments to out of gauge project cargo - we handle it all. Container drayage, High and Heavy, LTL, special equipment, drive-away, boat transport, vehicle shipping and much more.

Alaska service

Seattle is a major hub for most cargo moving to/from Alaska - we offer logistic services for all types of freight as well as transshipment handling. Terminal Operations, Transshipment, PickUp/Delivery, Transloading, Documentation, etc

Import

We can assist with any of your import shipments and handle all freight needs arriving to any port in US, where we can pick up and handle all the way down to final destination.

Warehousing

Warehousing Services, Container loading, cargo blocking/bracing/securing, storage, photo survey, container purchase, inspection, crating, packaging etc. Warehousing in all major cities in US and Canada.

Service to Russia

Over 10 years of exporting oil and gas equipment, mining equipment, oil well supplies, construction equipment, forestry machinery, vehicles, boats etc. Shipping to Russia is one of our main areas of expertise and we offer great service and rates.

Project Cargo

With many years in oil & gas industry - we have a vast experience handling export project cargo and delivering to various destinations worldwide.

Port/terminal handling

We work directly with all main Port Terminals in USA and Canada assisting with on-dock operations, crane lifts/hire, loading supervision, surveys and all cargo handling procedures that is required or needed.
